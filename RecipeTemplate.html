Egg and Vegetable Scramble

A quick, nutritious dish packed with protein and vitamins,perfect for
any meal of the day.Easy to make with minimal ingredients and effort!

Preparation Time:

  • Chopping & Beating: ~5 minutes
  • Cooking: ~7 minutes
  • Total Time: ~12 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • small tomato (chopped)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • A handful of spinach or any leafy green (optional)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon oil or butter

Instructions:

  1. Heat a pan with the oil or butter.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté until soft (about 2 minutes).
  3. Toss in the chopped tomato and spinach (if using) and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Beat the eggs in a bowl, add a pinch of salt, and pour them into the pan.
  5. Stir continuously until the eggs are fully cooked (2–3 minutes).
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
